We strictly value security. In this update, we've implemented:

1.  **Content Security Policy (CSP)**: Preventing XSS attacks.
2.  **Strict Transport Security (HSTS)**: Enforcing HTTPS.
3.  **X-Frame-Options**: Preventing clickjacking.
